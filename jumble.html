Main insight from research

People don't need more tracking — they need gentle reminders of what they already have.

Challenge

But... why does food waste happen?

A new enterprise brief got me thinking: sorting waste required too much thinking in fast everyday moments. People know they should sort — they just don't always know how, and the friction is enough to make the habit fall apart.

In response, I designed Sorta: a food sorting system paired with a companion app that helps users build better disposal habits through clarity, reinforcement, and reduced cognitive load.

Challenge

  • People forget what food they already have
  • Existing tools feel too complex or rigid
  • Food waste happens through small daily habits

Solution

  • Gentle awareness instead of strict tracking
  • Simple, low-friction interactions
  • Designed to fit into everyday routines
