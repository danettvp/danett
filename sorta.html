Overview

A new enterprise brief: I noticed how easy it was for food waste to become an afterthought. People wanted to dispose of waste correctly, but everyday systems made sustainable habits feel confusing, inconvenient, and easy to ignore. Sorting waste required too much thinking in fast everyday moments. In response, I designed Sorta: a food sorting system paired with a companion app that helps users build better disposal habits through clarity, reinforcement, and reduced cognitive load.

02 / Challenge & Solution

Challenge

  • People forget what food they already have
  • Existing tools feel too complex or rigid
  • Food waste happens through small daily habits

Solution

  • Gentle awareness instead of strict tracking
  • Simple, low-friction interactions
  • Designed to fit into everyday routines

Main insight from research

People don’t need more tracking — they need gentle reminders of what they already have.
